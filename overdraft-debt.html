Bank Overdraft Debt Help

Overdrafts are one of the most expensive ways to borrow in the UK. Many banks charge high daily fees or interest, and when you're constantly in the red, it becomes difficult to get out.

Why Are Overdrafts So Costly?

  • Daily interest charges or flat fees
  • No fixed repayment structure
  • Easy to dip deeper into debt

What Happens If I Can’t Repay My Overdraft?

If you can't bring your account back into credit, the bank may freeze your account, demand full repayment, or pass your case to a debt collection agency. Your credit score may also be affected.

Can I Include My Overdraft in a Debt Plan?

Yes. Bank overdraft debt can be included in most debt solutions including IVAs, DMPs, and DROs. We can help you freeze interest and repay in affordable monthly amounts.

Will the Bank Keep Charging Me?

Once you enter a debt solution, interest and charges are typically frozen, and your bank will stop further collections or pressure. Our team contacts them for you and handles the process.
