IVA vs DMP vs DRO â€” Which Route Fits Your Numbers?

IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

Formal, legally binding. One affordable payment; after successful completion, remaining eligible unsecured debt may be written off. Generally protects from most enforcement once approved.

DMP (Debt Management Plan)

Informal and flexible. One reduced payment to non-priority debts based on affordability. Interest reductions/freezes are often requested but not guaranteed.

DRO (Debt Relief Order)

For low income/low assets within set limits. Debts included are paused and may be written off after the period if criteria still apply.
