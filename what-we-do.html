What We Do at Fresh Start Debt Support

At Fresh Start, our mission is simple: to help people across the UK escape the stress of unaffordable debt. We specialise in connecting you with trusted solutions — quickly, professionally, and without pressure. Whether you're being chased by creditors or just struggling to keep up with payments, we’re here to make it easier.

We do not offer financial advice — instead, we help you understand your options and refer you to the right people when needed. Our approach is personal, friendly, and always tailored to your individual situation.

Our Core Services

  • ✅ Matching you with debt solutions like IVAs, DMPs, and DROs
  • ✅ Helping you stop interest, charges, and creditor contact
  • ✅ Supporting you through every step of the journey
  • ✅ Offering fast, discreet help without judgment

How We Help People Like You

Many people feel lost or ashamed when facing debt — but you’re not alone. Every day, we help individuals and families who are:

  • Struggling to make minimum payments
  • Being harassed by creditors or bailiffs
  • Falling behind on bills and rent
  • Worried about their credit rating or losing their home

We explain the solutions available clearly and without jargon. If you're eligible for something like an IVA or DMP, we’ll guide you through the steps to get set up quickly and professionally — often the same day.

Why People Choose Fresh Start

  • ✅ No call centre scripts — just real UK-based help
  • ✅ Quick assessments — no long forms or waiting weeks
  • ✅ We’ll fight to stop debt collectors and reduce payments
