Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

An IVA helps you repay debt through one affordable payment over 5â€“6 years, with remaining debt written off at the end. It stops interest, bailiffs, and legal action. Learn more on our IVA page.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

A DMP is an informal way to repay debts based on what you can afford. Interest may be frozen and creditor calls stopped. See how it works on our DMP page.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

If you have a low income and few assets, a DRO offers full debt write-off after 12 months with no payments. Find out if you qualify on our DRO page.

Bankruptcy and Trust Deeds

Bankruptcy is a last resort but can offer a full financial reset. In Scotland, a Trust Deed offers similar benefits to an IVA. Read more on our debt comparison guide.
