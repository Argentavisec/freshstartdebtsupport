What We Do at Fresh Start Debt Support

At Fresh Start, we help people across the UK deal with unaffordable debts by connecting them with trusted solutions such as IVAs, DMPs, and DROs. Our goal is to reduce stress, stop creditor contact, and give people a path to financial freedom — quickly and confidentially.

Our Mission

We’re not here to judge — we’re here to help. Whether you’ve fallen behind on credit cards, council tax, payday loans, or any other unsecured debt, we work hard to find a debt solution that fits your personal situation. Every case is different, and we treat it that way.

How We Help You

  • Explain your debt solution options in plain English
  • Check if you qualify for a government-backed IVA or DRO
  • Refer you to a licensed provider to handle your case
  • Help reduce monthly payments to something affordable
  • Stop contact from bailiffs and debt collectors
  • Support you through every step — with no pressure

Which Debt Solutions Can We Help With?

We can help you explore a range of options, including:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs)
  • Debt Management Plans (DMPs)
  • Debt Relief Orders (DROs)
  • Bankruptcy and other comparisons

If you’re not sure what’s right for you, that’s completely fine — our role is to help you understand your options without any pressure or obligation.
