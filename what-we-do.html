About Fresh Start Debt Support

At Fresh Start Debt Support, we’re dedicated to helping people across the UK regain financial freedom. Our experienced team provides personalised, compassionate guidance on debt solutions that align with your unique situation—focusing on IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement), DMP (Debt Management Plan), and other regulated debt relief methods.

Our Process

  • Start with a free, confidential consultation—to fully understand your situation.
  • Provide transparent advice on your options: IVA, DMP, DRO, bankruptcy.
  • Prepare and submit the plan proposal or application.
  • Guide and support you throughout repayments or monitoring periods.
  • Help you rebuild—credit-wise, financially, and emotionally.
