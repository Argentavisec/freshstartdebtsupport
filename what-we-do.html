What We Do

At Fresh Start Debt Support, we help people across the UK break free from the burden of debt by offering clear, professional, and confidential support. Our mission is to give you the tools and guidance you need to take control of your finances — with no judgement, no pressure, and no hidden fees.

Our Services Include:

  • Debt Assessment – We review your financial situation to understand the best options available to you.
  • Debt Management Plans (DMPs) – Ideal for those who can repay debt with a more affordable monthly payment.
  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs) – Write off a portion of your debt and make one affordable payment over time.
  • Creditor Negotiation – We work with your creditors to stop interest, freeze charges, and stop collections.
  • Ongoing Support – You're never alone. Our advisors are here to help you every step of the way.

Whether you’re employed, self-employed, or receiving benefits, we tailor our support to your personal circumstances. Our goal is to reduce stress, simplify your finances, and provide you with real options.
