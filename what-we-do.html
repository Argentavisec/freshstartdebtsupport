About Fresh Start Debt Support

Fresh Start Debt Support was created to help everyday people take back control of their finances. We understand how overwhelming it can feel to deal with credit card debt, catalogue debt, or constant contact from debt collectors.

Our goal is to make the path forward simple, supportive, and stress-free.

We’re not a large call centre. We work with fewer people so we can give you more attention and a tailored service. That means faster help, honest answers, and a real person guiding you from day one — no judgment, no pressure, and no obligation.

We believe in second chances. Whether you’re behind on council tax, facing bailiff action, or just need someone to explain your options clearly, we’re here to help you move forward with confidence.
