What We Do at Fresh Start Debt Support

Fresh Start Debt Support is here to help individuals across the UK deal with unaffordable debt — clearly, privately, and without judgment. If you're feeling overwhelmed by bills or struggling to keep up with loan, credit card, or council tax payments, you’re not alone — and you don’t have to face it by yourself.

We are not financial advisers. Instead, we help you understand your options and connect you with a qualified specialist who can take it from there. Our role is to simplify the process and support you as you move toward a debt-free future.

How Our Process Works

  • Step 1 – Quick Online Assessment: Use our free debt calculator or Check If You Qualify form to tell us about your situation.
  • Step 2 – We Review Your Info: Our small team privately reviews your debt level, income, and circumstances to understand what might help.
  • Step 3 – You’re Connected: If you meet the criteria, we’ll pass you to a licensed specialist who can offer a debt solution tailored to your needs.
  • Step 4 – You Decide: There’s never any obligation. You stay in control of what to do next.

Popular Debt Solutions We Help With

We regularly help people who are struggling with:

  • IVAs (Individual Voluntary Arrangements) – write off unaffordable debt over 5–6 years
  • Debt Management Plans (DMPs) – flexible payment plans with frozen interest
  • Debt Relief Orders (DROs) – for people on low income with no assets
  • Bankruptcy – a legal option to clear debts in serious cases
  • Trust Deeds – the Scottish equivalent of an IVA

Not sure which one fits your situation? Check If You Qualify and we’ll help guide you through it.

Benefits of Getting Help with Your Debts

  • ✅ Combine all debts into one affordable monthly payment
  • ✅ Stop interest and charges from piling up
  • ✅ Prevent further contact from creditors and bailiffs
  • ✅ Write off a portion of your debt (if eligible)
