Help with Store Card Debt

Store cards are often offered as a convenient way to shop, but their high interest rates can lead to growing debt. If you're struggling to repay multiple store cards, Fresh Start Debt Support is here to help you take control.

Why Is Store Card Debt a Problem?

  • Very high interest rates (often 25% or more)
  • Missed payments can lead to late fees and damage your credit score
  • Many people are pressured into taking out multiple store cards

Can Store Card Debt Be Written Off?

Yes. If you're eligible for an IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement), a portion of your store card debt could be written off. A DMP (Debt Management Plan) can help reduce repayments and freeze interest.

What Options Do I Have?

  • IVA: Write off unaffordable debt, pay one affordable amount monthly
  • DMP: Informal plan with lower payments
  • DRO: Suitable if you have low income and assets

How Do I Stop the Interest?

Most creditors will freeze interest and charges once you enter a supported plan like an IVA or DMP. We’ll communicate with creditors for you.

Will This Affect My Credit Score?

Yes, debt solutions will appear on your credit file, but the long-term benefit of becoming debt-free often outweighs this. Continuing to fall behind on payments will also damage your score.

When Should I Act?

Now. The earlier you seek help, the more options you'll have. Don’t let store card debt grow out of control.
