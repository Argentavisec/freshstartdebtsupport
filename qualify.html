Check If You Qualify for Help

If you’re struggling with debt, you’re not alone — and there may be a solution for you.

Do You Qualify for a Debt Solution?

You may qualify for support like an IVA or Debt Management Plan if you:

  • Owe £6,000 or more in unsecured debts
  • Have 2 or more creditors (like credit cards or loans)
  • Live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • Have a regular income or receive benefits
  • Want to stop interest and creditor contact
