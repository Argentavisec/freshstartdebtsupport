DRO vs IVA vs DMP vs Bankruptcy

  • DRO: For low income/few assets; write-off after ~12 months if criteria persist
  • DMP: Informal, flexible repayment across non-priority debts
  • IVA: Formal, 1 affordable payment; write-off at completion (typically 5‚Äì6 years)
  • Bankruptcy: Fast discharge but heavier implications

What Debts Can a DRO Include?

Commonly included:

  • Credit & store cards, personal loans, overdrafts
  • Catalogues & BNPL (e.g., Klarna)
  • Utility arrears and council tax debt
  • Payday loans and high-cost short-term credit

Usually excluded: secured debts (mortgage, HP on car), court fines, student loans. We‚Äôll check each account for you.
