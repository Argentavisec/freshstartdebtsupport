Frequently Asked Questions About Debt

Will entering a debt solution affect my credit score?

Yes, entering an IVA, DMP, or bankruptcy will affect your credit rating. However, these solutions are designed to help you resolve your debt long-term.

How long do debt solutions last?

IVAs usually last 5 years, DMPs are flexible and depend on how much you owe, and bankruptcy generally lasts 1 year.

Will creditors stop contacting me?

Yes. If you enter a formal solution like an IVA, we inform your creditors and they’re legally required to stop all contact and collection efforts.

Can I keep my car if I enter a debt solution?

Yes, in most cases you can keep your vehicle if it’s essential for work or family responsibilities.

What debts can be included?

Unsecured debts such as credit cards, personal loans, overdrafts, council tax arrears, payday loans, and more.
