Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IVA?

An IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) is a legal agreement with your creditors to repay a portion of your debts over a set period, usually 5–6 years. After that, any remaining debt may be written off.

What is a DMP?

A Debt Management Plan is an informal arrangement to repay your debts in affordable monthly payments. It is flexible but not legally binding, and it may not freeze all interest and charges.

Will debt solutions affect my credit score?

Yes. Most debt solutions will show on your credit report and may lower your score during the plan. However, they can help you rebuild credit after completion by clearing problem debt.

Can I keep my home?

Yes. Many debt solutions (like IVAs or DMPs) allow you to protect your home, though you may need to release some equity if applicable. Bankruptcy could impact your property in some cases.

Will creditors still contact me?

In an IVA, creditor contact must stop by law. With a DMP, we work to negotiate with creditors, though contact may still occur early in the process.

How long does it take to set up?

We can set up most debt solutions in as little as 24–72 hours once we receive your details. Our team handles everything to make the process stress-free.
