Solutions That Can Include Utility Bills Debt

Rolling your energy, water and other unsecured debts into one plan keeps life simple and reduces the risk of fresh arrears elsewhere.

  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – informal, flexible and widely used. Many creditors freeze or reduce interest/charges.
  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – formal, legally binding, single monthly payment. Remaining eligible unsecured debt may be written off at the end.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – for low income/low assets. Debts are frozen for 12 months and then written off if your situation hasn’t improved.

Unsure which route fits your budget and goals? Read side-by-side differences at Compare Solutions or explore the specifics of an IVA and a DMP.

Priority vs Non-Priority: Where Do Utility Bills Fit In?

In most budgets, energy and water are treated as essential expenditure. If you’re also behind on council tax or rent/mortgage, you must protect the roof over your head and household utilities first. Then, use a structured plan to share a fair amount among all unsecured creditors.

  • 🏠 Cover housing (rent/mortgage) and council tax first.
  • ⚡ Keep energy and water running—agree a realistic plan.
  • 💳 Share an affordable amount across cards, loans, catalogues via DMP or IVA.

This approach prevents short-term crises and sets you up for long-term success.

Can Utility Bills Debt Be Written Off?

In some cases, unaffordable utility arrears can be written off as part of a formal arrangement:

Utility Bills Debt FAQs

Can my energy or water supplier cut me off for arrears?

Suppliers have processes for dealing with non-payment, but you have options to avoid the worst outcomes. The earlier you agree a realistic plan, the better. If collectors are already involved, start with Stop Debt Collectors.

Is there help if I can’t afford both utilities and debts?

Yes. Prioritise essentials (housing, council tax, utilities) and then share a fair amount across other creditors via a DMP or IVA. Use the Debt Calculator to find a sustainable payment.

Can utility bills debt be included in an IVA or DRO?

Often, yes—subject to eligibility and your overall situation. Learn more about IVA and DRO, or compare options at Compare Solutions.

Will a DMP stop interest and charges?

Many creditors will freeze or reduce charges within a DMP, but it’s not guaranteed and relies on creditor cooperation. A formal IVA typically freezes interest and sets an agreed affordable payment.

I’m in my overdraft every month—what can I do?

Overdraft fees can drain the funds you need for utilities. Read Bank Overdrafts to see how to restructure and stop the cycle.

Debt collectors are threatening legal action—what now?

First, understand your rights: What is debt collection? Then follow de-escalation steps at Stop Debt Collectors. If particular firms are contacting you, check our guides (e.g., Lowell, Moorcroft, Cabot).

Will an IVA or DRO affect my credit file?

Yes. Formal solutions affect your credit for a period, but they can also stop pressure, protect essentials and give a structured path out of debt. Compare the trade-offs at Compare Solutions.
