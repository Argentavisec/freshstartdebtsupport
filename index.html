What is a Debt Management Plan (DMP)?

A Debt Management Plan (DMP) is an informal and flexible way to repay your unsecured debts at a pace that suits your current financial situation. It’s suitable for individuals who have steady income but are struggling with repayment terms or interest rates.

Managed by trusted debt advisors, a DMP allows you to consolidate your monthly payments into one affordable amount. There's no court involvement, no pressure, and often no setup fees.

  • Lower monthly payments based on your actual income
  • Freeze or reduce interest and charges
  • One monthly payment to cover all creditors
  • No legal obligation — it’s non-binding and voluntary
  • No upfront fees, no surprises
  • Stay out of court and avoid enforcement action

Our team will handle communication with your creditors, remove the stress of constant letters and calls, and give you the time and breathing room to get back on track.
