Breathing Space ‚Äî Pause Interest, Charges & Most Enforcement

What it does

In England & Wales, the Breathing Space (Debt Respite Scheme) pauses interest, charges and most enforcement on qualifying debts while you work with an adviser to set up a plan. Eligibility & Key Updates (IVA, DMP, DRO)

IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

Typically suits unsecured debts around ¬£6,000+ with a steady income. Interest/enforcement are usually frozen once approved, and remaining qualifying balances may be written off at completion (5‚Äì6 years).

DMP (Debt Management Plan)

Informal and flexible ‚Äî one reduced payment to non-priority debts based on your budget. Interest freezes are requested but not guaranteed.

DRO (Debt Relief Order)

Designed for low income and low assets within strict limits. The ¬£90 DRO fee was abolished (April 2024), and eligibility thresholds were increased in June 2024. This has made DROs more accessible for many people.
