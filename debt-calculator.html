How Our Debt Calculator Works

To get an estimate, our tool will ask for key financial details — such as:

  • Your total unsecured debt (e.g. credit cards, loans, overdrafts)
  • Your current income and monthly living expenses
  • Your employment status and number of creditors

Based on your answers, we’ll show a likely monthly payment amount, and which solutions you may be eligible for:

  • IVA – Pay what you can afford for 5–6 years, write off remaining debt
  • DMP – Informal agreement to repay debts in full over time
  • DRO – Government-backed relief if you owe less than £30,000 and have no assets

Example: Jane from Birmingham

Debts: £14,200 (credit cards, catalogue, payday loans)
Monthly income: £1,600
Essential living costs: £1,200

Using the calculator, Jane found she could afford around £150/month. She qualified for an IVA and is now writing off over £9,000 of debt after 60 months of affordable payments.
