Answering High-Intent UK Searches

“Debt affordability calculator UK” — what’s a safe payment?

Your safe payment is what remains after essential bills and realistic living costs. Our calculator gives a guide; we then validate against statements so creditors accept it and interest reduction is more likely.

“IVA write-off calculator £70 a month” — could I qualify?

Some IVAs are approved at lower contributions if the budget supports it. Try the estimator above, then we’ll check eligibility and creditor appetite for your case.

“DMP payment calculator UK” — one affordable payment

A DMP is informal and flexible. Many lenders freeze interest when you show a fair, consistent payment based on a verified budget.

“Breathing Space calculator / how much relief do I get?”

It’s not a money amount — it’s legal protection: creditors pause interest, most fees and most enforcement while you set up a plan with an adviser.

“Stop bailiffs fast” — how quickly can pressure ease?

Once protections apply (Breathing Space or a formal solution), most new enforcement is paused. We’ll contact creditors in writing and move you into the right route swiftly.

Debt Calculator — FAQs

Will using the calculator affect my credit score?

No. It’s just guidance. Formal solutions have credit impacts during the term, but they stop things worsening and give a clear finish line.

Ask About Credit Impact

Is the estimate guaranteed?

It’s an estimate. We make it accurate by confirming income, bills and spending from your bank statements and using fair allowances so creditors accept it.

Get a Verified Budget

How long does an IVA last?

Usually 5–6 years. In some cases an extra 12 months replaces equity release if you own a home.

Read More About IVAs

Has the £90 DRO fee been scrapped?

Yes, in April 2024, making DROs more accessible if you meet strict low-income/asset limits.

What if I’m in Scotland?

Different options apply (e.g., temporary moratorium, Trust Deeds, DAS). Tell us your postcode and we’ll signpost correctly.
