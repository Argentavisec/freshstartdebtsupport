Understanding Your Debt Solution Options

Once you have an idea of what you might afford each month, it‚Äôs important to understand the debt solutions available:

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

IVAs are formal, legally binding agreements where you make monthly payments over 5-6 years, after which remaining qualifying debt can be written off. They provide legal protection from creditors and bailiffs.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

DMPs offer flexible repayment plans based on what you can afford. They are informal, so creditors can still take legal action, but often agree to freeze interest and charges.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

DROs help people with low income and minimal assets by freezing debts for 12 months, after which debts may be written off if circumstances remain unchanged.

Bankruptcy

Bankruptcy is a formal insolvency solution that discharges debts usually after 12 months. It has a significant impact on credit but can offer a fresh start.

Trust Deed (Scotland)

Trust Deeds are formal agreements in Scotland that provide debt write-off after typically 4 years, with legal protections during that time.
