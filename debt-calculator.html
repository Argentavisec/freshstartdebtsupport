Debt Calculator — FAQs

Will using the calculator affect my credit score?

No. It’s just guidance. Formal solutions may impact your credit during the term, but they stop the spiral and provide a clear finish line. See Credit Score Help.

Is the estimate guaranteed?

It’s an estimate. We make it accurate by confirming income, bills and spending from your bank statements and using fair allowances so creditors accept it. Compare options at Compare Solutions.

How long does an IVA last?

Usually 5–6 years. In some cases an extra 12 months replaces equity release if you own a home. Read more on IVAs.

Has the £90 DRO fee been scrapped?

Yes — from April 2024, making DROs more accessible if you meet strict low-income/asset limits.

What if I’m in Scotland?

Different options apply (e.g., temporary moratorium, Trust Deeds, DAS). Tell us your postcode and we’ll signpost correctly. See Trust Deed (Scotland).
