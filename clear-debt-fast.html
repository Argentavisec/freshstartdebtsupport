Ways to Clear Debt Fast in the UK

The fastest way to clear debt depends on your income, debt level, and personal circumstances. Below are some of the most effective UK debt solutions:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – Write off up to 80% of your debt and make one affordable monthly payment for 5 years
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – Ideal for people with low income and debts under £30,000. Debt can be cleared after just 12 months with no payments
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – Flexible solution that allows you to reduce payments and freeze interest informally
  • Compare All Solutions – See how each option works and find your best fit

Each of these options is designed to help you stop creditor contact, avoid legal action, and reduce financial stress — fast.

What Debts Can Be Cleared?

You can clear many types of debt quickly with the right plan. These include:

Most unsecured debts can be included in a formal plan. In many cases, enforcement action and interest are stopped once a solution is in place.

If you’re falling behind or being chased by creditors, acting quickly can make a huge difference. The sooner a plan is in place, the sooner the pressure stops.

How Fast Can Debt Be Cleared?

Some people clear debts in as little as 12 months with a DRO. Others may use a longer-term option like an IVA to gradually pay what they can afford and have the rest written off after 5–6 years.
