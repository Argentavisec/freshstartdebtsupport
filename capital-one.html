Falling behind on your Capital One credit card? You’re not alone. Thousands of people in the UK face mounting interest, late fees, and pressure from Capital One collections. The good news is — you have options.

At Fresh Start Debt Support, we help people across the UK take back control of their finances with simple, affordable debt solutions — and that includes help with Capital One debts.

What Happens If You Can’t Pay Capital One?

  • ⚠️ Late fees and high interest charges may be added
  • ⚠️ Your credit file may be damaged with a default notice
  • ⚠️ The debt could be passed to collectors like Lowell or Link Financial
  • ⚠️ You may face repeated calls, letters, or legal action

Don't wait for things to escalate. We can help you explore formal solutions that stop interest, freeze collections, and simplify repayments.

How to Stop Capital One Interest and Charges

You may be able to freeze your Capital One interest and reduce payments through the following options:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – One affordable monthly payment, with remaining debt written off after 5–6 years
  • Debt Management Plan (DMP) – Lower payments and stop creditor contact, without going to court
  • Debt Relief Order (DRO) – For those on low income, with debts cleared in 12 months

Use our free debt calculator to check which options you may be eligible for — no obligation.

Has Capital One Sold Your Debt?

If your Capital One account has been passed to a debt collector, we can still help. Some of the most common collection agencies we see include:

  • Lowell Financial
  • PRA Group
  • Capquest Debt Recovery

Once your debt is in collections, you may still be eligible for a formal plan that pauses contact and consolidates your debt into one reduced monthly payment.
