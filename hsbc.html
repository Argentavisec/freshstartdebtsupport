Options for HSBC Debt: Choose a Route That Fits Your Life

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

Informal, flexible and based on what you can genuinely afford. Many creditors reduce or freeze interest/charges (not guaranteed). Good if you need adaptability and want to avoid formal insolvency.

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

A formal, legally binding agreement. One affordable monthly payment; if you complete successfully, remaining eligible unsecured debt may be written off. Strong protection from most creditor action.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

Designed for low income/low assets. Debts are normally frozen for 12 months and then written off if your situation hasn‚Äôt improved. Strict eligibility applies.
