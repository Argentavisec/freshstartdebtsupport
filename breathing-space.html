Eligibility & Conditions: Do You Qualify?

Standard Breathing Space (60 days)

  • Live or usually reside in England or Wales
  • Have at least one qualifying debt
  • Not currently in a DRO/IVA/bankruptcy/interim order
  • Not used a standard Breathing Space in the last 12 months
  • Engage with debt advice during the moratorium

Mental Health Crisis Breathing Space (MH-CBS)

Available while you‚Äôre receiving qualifying mental health crisis treatment; protections last for the treatment period plus 30 days. There is no 12-month limit on how often you can access MH-CBS.

What Breathing Space Covers ‚Äî and What Continues

Protections you get

What continues

Mental Health Crisis Breathing Space (During Treatment)

How it works

Protections last for the duration of qualifying mental health crisis treatment plus 30 days. An Approved Mental Health Professional or other authorised healthcare contact verifies treatment so your adviser can initiate protections.

Why it helps
  • Stops most enforcement on included debts while you receive treatment
  • Freezes most interest/charges during the period
  • No limit on the number of MH-CBS entries (unlike standard 12-month rule)
