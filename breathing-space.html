What Debts Are Covered by the Breathing Space Scheme?

The scheme applies to most common unsecured debts, including:

  • Credit cards, overdrafts, and payday loans
  • Utility bills such as gas, electric, and water
  • Council tax arrears
  • Personal loans and catalog debts
  • HMRC debts like income tax and National Insurance
  • Benefit overpayments (e.g. Universal Credit)

However, Breathing Space does not cover:

  • Secured loans like mortgages and car finance
  • Student loans
  • Court fines or criminal debts

Even if your debts are not covered, the scheme can help pause enforcement on eligible debts, giving you vital relief.

What Happens When Your Breathing Space Ends?

Breathing Space provides temporary relief. Once the period ends, creditors can resume contact and enforcement unless you have set up a longer-term solution such as:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)
  • Debt Management Plan (DMP)
  • Debt Relief Order (DRO)
  • Bankruptcy

Our team works with you during Breathing Space to prepare these solutions, so you don‚Äôt face the end of the pause unprotected.

Breathing Space Frequently Asked Questions

Is Breathing Space available in Scotland or Northern Ireland?

No. The scheme is currently only available in England and Wales. However, Scotland has other debt help options such as Trust Deeds.

Does Breathing Space affect my credit rating?

No. Breathing Space does not appear on your credit report, though missed payments prior to entry may have already impacted your score.

Can I get Breathing Space more than once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple periods of Breathing Space if you continue to meet the eligibility requirements.

Will creditors contact me during Breathing Space?

No. All creditor contact must stop during your Breathing Space period, including calls, letters, and enforcement visits.

Is there a cost for entering Breathing Space?

No. The initial Breathing Space application and advice from Fresh Start Debt Support are free. Charges may apply for long-term solutions after Breathing Space ends.
