Solutions for Credit Card Debt in the UK

Depending on your situation, you could qualify for a number of formal or informal solutions including:

IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

An IVA allows you to make one affordable monthly payment for 5–6 years. The rest of your debt – including interest and charges – may be written off at the end. You’ll be legally protected from creditors.

DMP (Debt Management Plan)

A DMP is an informal plan where your repayments are based on what you can afford. Many creditors agree to freeze interest and charges, helping you pay off your debts faster and more affordably.

DRO (Debt Relief Order)

If you owe under £30,000 and have little income or assets, a DRO could write off your debts completely after 12 months. Strict criteria apply, but it can be a powerful option.
