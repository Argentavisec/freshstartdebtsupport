What Happens If I Can’t Pay My Credit Card Debt?

Missing payments can quickly lead to:

  • ✅ Additional interest and late fees
  • ✅ Damage to your credit score
  • ✅ Persistent contact from creditors or debt collectors
  • ✅ Legal action in some cases

But help is available. We support people every day who have fallen behind — even by thousands of pounds — and guide them toward a fresh financial start using trusted debt solutions like IVAs, DMPs, and DROs.

Debt Solutions for Credit Card Debt

If you're behind on payments, you may qualify for one of the following solutions:

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

With an IVA, you could write off a portion of your debt and make one affordable monthly payment over 5–6 years. It offers legal protection from creditors and bailiffs.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

A DMP allows you to repay your credit card debt at a pace you can afford. We'll negotiate with your creditors to freeze interest and charges where possible.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

If you owe less than £30,000 and have few assets, a DRO can help write off your debt after 12 months — with no payments required in that time.
