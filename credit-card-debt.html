Struggling with Credit Card Debt?

You're not alone. Millions of people in the UK are dealing with growing balances, rising interest, and constant creditor calls. At Fresh Start Debt Support, we offer solutions that can reduce your monthly repayments, freeze interest, and give you back control.

How Does Credit Card Debt Build Up?

Credit card debt often starts small but can grow rapidly due to high interest rates. Missed payments, only paying the minimum, and relying on credit for everyday expenses can quickly lead to balances spiraling out of control.

What Are the Risks of Unpaid Credit Card Debt?

  • Constant calls and letters from creditors or debt collectors
  • Damage to your credit score, affecting future borrowing
  • Legal action such as County Court Judgments (CCJs)
  • Rising balances due to added interest and charges

What Help is Available for Credit Card Debt?

Fresh Start can help you access formal and informal solutions, including:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – write off unaffordable debt, make one payment, and freeze interest
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – repay at your own pace without legal pressure
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – a low-cost insolvency solution for those with low income and assets

How Much Credit Card Debt Can I Write Off?

Depending on your financial situation and the solution you qualify for, you could write off up to 85% of your credit card debt with an IVA. In a DMP, while you repay everything, interest and charges may be frozen to make it affordable.

How Long Does It Take to Clear My Debt?

With an IVA, most clients complete the agreement in 5 to 6 years. A DMP is flexible and continues until your debt is fully repaid. DROs last 12 months and then write off qualifying debts if your situation hasn’t changed.

Can I Stop the Interest and Creditor Calls?

Yes. Many creditors agree to freeze interest once you enter a formal solution like an IVA or a supported DMP. Our team will also handle all communication with creditors so you don’t have to deal with the stress.
