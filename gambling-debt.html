Help With Gambling Debt

Gambling debt can feel overwhelming and isolating, but you're not alone. Whether you've used credit to fund gambling or found yourself in arrears due to losses, help is available. At Fresh Start, we offer confidential, judgment-free support to guide you toward a solution.

What Is Gambling Debt?

Gambling debt can result from using loans, credit cards, payday lenders, or even overdrafts to fund betting or gaming activity. It often builds quickly and can spiral due to interest, fees, and pressure from creditors.

Can Gambling Debt Be Written Off?

Yes — if you qualify for an IVA or DRO, a portion of your gambling-related debt could be written off after a set period. Our team can assess your situation to determine if you qualify.

Will I Be Judged?

No. We treat every enquiry with compassion and confidentiality. We understand how gambling can impact lives and we're here to help, not judge.

What Are My Options?

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement): Reduce debts into one affordable payment and write off the rest after 5 years.
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan): Lower your monthly repayments and freeze interest without taking out more credit.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order): Suitable if you owe less than £30,000 and have minimal assets.
