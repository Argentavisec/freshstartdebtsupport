What is an IVA?

An Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) is a formal debt solution that lets you reduce your monthly payments and write off a significant portion of your debt. It’s legally binding and protects you from creditor contact, interest, charges, and legal action.

Who Qualifies for an IVA?

You may be eligible for an IVA if you:

  • Owe over £6,000 in unsecured debt
  • Have debts with two or more creditors
  • Live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • Have a regular source of income from employment, self-employment, or benefits

Not sure if you qualify? Our debt specialists can give you free, no-pressure guidance based on your circumstances.

What Debts Can Be Included in an IVA?

You can include a wide range of unsecured debts in an IVA, including:

  • Credit card debt
  • Personal loans
  • Overdrafts
  • Catalogue accounts
  • Payday loans
  • Council tax arrears
  • Gas, electric, and water bills
  • Store cards
  • HMRC debts
