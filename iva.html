What is an IVA?

An Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) is a formal and legally binding agreement between you and your creditors to repay your debts at an affordable monthly rate — usually over a 5 or 6 year period. It is designed to help people with £6,000 or more in unsecured debts who are struggling to meet current repayment terms.

Benefits of an IVA

  • Write off up to 85% of your debt upon successful completion
  • One affordable monthly payment based on your income
  • Stop creditor contact and freeze interest & charges
  • Legally protects your home and assets
  • No upfront fees — managed by licensed insolvency practitioners

Once your IVA is approved, all creditors included in the plan must legally stop pursuing you. You’ll have peace of mind knowing your debt is under control and that you’re on a path toward becoming debt free.

Who is Eligible?

IVAs are typically available to UK residents with over £6,000 of unsecured debt (like credit cards, loans, store cards, or payday loans). You must also have a regular income and be able to commit to monthly payments.
