What is an IVA?

An IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) is a legal agreement between you and your creditors to repay debts over a set period, usually 5 years. It helps you write off a large portion of debt and consolidate payments into one affordable monthly amount.

Why choose an IVA?

  • Write off up to 85% of unsecured debts
  • Freeze interest and stop creditor contact
  • Protect your home and assets
  • Flexible and regulated by UK insolvency law

Our team will assess your eligibility and walk you through the IVA process from start to finish.
