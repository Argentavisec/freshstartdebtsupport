What is an IVA?

An IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) is a formal agreement with your creditors to repay part of your debt in affordable monthly installments — usually over a period of five years. After that, any remaining debt can be legally written off.

This government-approved debt solution is ideal for UK residents with over £6,000 in unsecured debt who can commit to regular repayments.

  • Write off up to 85% of your unsecured debt
  • Freeze interest and charges immediately
  • Stop creditor contact and legal action
  • Protect your home, vehicle and wages from enforcement
  • Affordable monthly payments based on your actual budget
