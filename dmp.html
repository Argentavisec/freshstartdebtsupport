What is a Debt Management Plan (DMP)?

A Debt Management Plan (DMP) is an informal solution that allows you to repay your unsecured debts in affordable monthly installments. It’s ideal for people with regular income who are struggling with high-interest repayments but want to avoid court-based solutions.

Key Benefits of a DMP

  • Lower your monthly repayments to match your income and expenses
  • Freeze interest and charges (subject to creditor agreement)
  • No court involvement or legal proceedings
  • No setup fees with Fresh Start Debt Support
  • Combine multiple debts into one manageable payment
  • Keep control of your finances while rebuilding your credit profile

Our experienced team will handle all creditor communication and help you feel confident about your progress. You stay in full control, without legal obligations or pressure.

Is a DMP Right for You?

A DMP may be suitable if you:

  • Owe less than £6,000 in total debts
  • Want to avoid formal insolvency like an IVA or bankruptcy
  • Can commit to regular monthly payments
