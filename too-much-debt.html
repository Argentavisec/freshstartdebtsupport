How Do I Know If I Have Too Much Debt?

Debt becomes “too much” when it disrupts your financial stability and daily life. Warning signs include:

  • 📉 Only paying the minimum on credit cards
  • 💳 Borrowing more just to make repayments
  • 📞 Receiving letters or calls from collectors or bailiffs
  • 🏠 Missing rent, mortgage, council tax, or utility bills
  • 😔 Stress, anxiety, or sleepless nights about money

If one or more apply, you’re carrying too much debt — but solutions exist.

What Debts Can Be Written Off?

Depending on your eligibility, you may write off some or all of these:

  • 💳 Credit cards, loans, overdrafts
  • 🏘️ Council tax arrears
  • ⚡ Gas, electricity, water bills
  • 📱 Mobile contracts & catalogues
  • 🚗 Car finance shortfalls
  • 💰 Payday loans
  • 📑 Benefit overpayments
  • 🚨 Parking fines & penalty charges

Not usually written off: student loans, child maintenance, court fines. We’ll guide you through the details.
