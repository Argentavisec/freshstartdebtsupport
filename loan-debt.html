What Happens If I Can’t Pay My Personal Loan?

If you fall behind on personal loan repayments, the lender may add charges or default interest. In many cases, your debt may be passed to a debt collection agency like Lowell Financial or Moriarty Law. This can damage your credit file and lead to legal action such as County Court Judgments (CCJs).

We work with people in your situation every day and can help you pause the pressure through the Breathing Space scheme, or find a longer-term debt solution.

Solutions to Personal Loan Debt

You might qualify for one of the following UK debt solutions:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement): Reduce your personal loan repayments to one affordable monthly payment and write off remaining debt after 5 years.
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan): An informal plan to lower payments and freeze interest. Great for personal loans from banks and online lenders.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order): Suitable for people with low income and little to no assets. Ideal for small personal loan debts.
