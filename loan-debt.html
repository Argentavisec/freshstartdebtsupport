IVA, DMP or DRO — Which Fits Personal Loan Debt?

IVA — formal solution

Best where debts are around £6,000+ and income is steady. Once approved, interest and most enforcement are usually frozen; any remaining qualifying unsecured debt may be written off at completion (typically after 5–6 years).

DMP — informal, flexible

One reduced payment to non-priority debts, reviewed against a fair budget. Interest freezes are requested but not guaranteed; many lenders cooperate when offers are realistic.

DRO — strict criteria

Suited to low income and low assets within set limits. Qualifying debts are legally paused and may be written off after the DRO period if criteria still apply.

Personal Loan Help — Search-Intent Topics

Freeze interest & charges on a personal loan

Show a verified budget and a fair proposal. Many lenders will consider stopping interest/charges when an affordable plan is in place. If suitable, Breathing Space can pause most interest and enforcement while that plan is arranged.

Personal loan default — what it means

A default is normally recorded after persistent arrears. It can lead to collection activity or a court claim. Seek advice early — protections and realistic offers can prevent escalation and protect essentials.

Token payments on personal loans

When affordability is very tight, short-term token offers can reduce pressure while you gather documents, enter protections or move into a longer-term option such as a DMP, IVA or DRO.

Including a personal loan in an IVA/DMP

Personal loans are unsecured and commonly included in both IVAs and DMPs. We’ll confirm eligibility and which route best fits your income stability and goals.

Personal Loan Debt — FAQs

Can my personal loan be included in an IVA?

Yes. Personal loans are unsecured and commonly included. Once approved, most enforcement and interest are usually frozen.

Will Breathing Space stop calls and letters?

During the protection period on qualifying debts, creditors should pause interest/charges and most enforcement and should not contact you about enforcement.

Is a DMP enough for a single personal loan?

Often, yes — if the plan is affordable and verified. If affordability is very low or debts are high, an IVA or DRO may be safer.

Will this hurt my credit file?

Breathing Space itself isn’t an insolvency entry, but payment history still matters. Formal solutions impact credit during the term while giving a route to stability.

What if I’m in Scotland?

Different options apply (e.g., temporary moratorium, Trust Deeds, DAS). Tell us your postcode and we’ll signpost correctly.

How quickly can I get protected?

Once we have the basics (debtor info, creditor list, balances and a verified budget), protections may begin quickly if suitable. We’ll prioritise urgent cases.
