Help with Personal Loan Debt in the UK

Are your personal loan repayments becoming too much to handle? Whether you’ve borrowed from high street banks, online lenders, or credit unions, falling behind can quickly lead to financial stress, mounting charges, and even legal action. At Fresh Start Debt Support, we help people across the UK find a way out of personal loan debt — fast, safely, and confidentially.

What Happens If You Can’t Afford Your Personal Loan?

  • Missed payments may result in default charges, penalty interest, or your loan being passed to debt collectors like Lowell Financial
  • Accounts can be marked as defaulted on your credit report
  • Some lenders may start legal action or apply for a County Court Judgment (CCJ)

Can Personal Loan Debt Be Reduced or Written Off?

Yes — if you qualify for a formal debt solution like an Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA), you could write off a significant portion of your unsecured loan debt. With an IVA, your monthly payment is based on what you can afford, and creditors stop interest and legal action. After 5 years, any remaining balances may be written off completely.

Struggling With Multiple Loans?

Many of our clients have more than one loan or a combination of debts, such as credit card balances, payday loans, or even overdrafts. We can help you combine these into one affordable monthly payment through an informal DMP or formal IVA, depending on your situation.

Can I Stop Interest and Creditor Contact?

Yes. When you enter an approved plan like an IVA or DMP, interest is usually frozen and contact from lenders stops. If your loan has already been sold to a collector like Moriarty Law or PRA Group, we’ll handle communication and stop the harassment.

Do I Need to Be Behind on Payments to Get Help?

No — if you’re struggling or worried you’re about to fall behind, now is the best time to act. We offer early intervention to stop things getting worse, and all advice is completely free and confidential.
