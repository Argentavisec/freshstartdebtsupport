Single Mum Struggling with Debt? Pause Pressure, Then Plan Properly

As a single mother, costs like childcare, food and travel can make budgets tight. We help you: (1) enter Breathing Space to pause interest, charges and most enforcement on qualifying debts; (2) set a realistic plan across all creditors — often a DMP, an IVA or a DRO if eligible. We’ll build a defendable budget that safeguards essentials first.

Quick script for creditors (single mum):
“I’m a single parent in financial difficulty. I’m working with a debt adviser and need an affordable temporary arrangement on my [credit card/loan/overdraft]. Please review interest/fees and accept a fair payment while my plan is being set up.”

Open all letters and keep copies. If you receive a “Letter of Claim” or court forms, respond within the deadline and contact us the same day.

Priority Bills First — Council Tax, Energy, Rent/Mortgage

We protect essentials first: home, heat, lights, water and children’s needs. That reduces risk and makes creditors more likely to accept your plan. Then we divide a fair, consistent payment across unsecured debts.

Credit Cards & Overdrafts — Reduce Fees, One Payment

A direct arrangement can help short-term, but a DMP consolidates multiple debts into one affordable payment. Many lenders freeze or reduce interest when your budget is realistic and verified (not guaranteed).

What creditors need to see

  • Priority bills funded first, including childcare
  • Consistent, fair offers across all creditors
  • Clear evidence (statements, bills) and ongoing communication

FAQs — Single Mums & Debt

Will debt solutions affect my benefits?

Debt solutions aren’t the same as income benefits. We’ll make sure your budget reflects the correct benefit amounts and essential costs; we’ll also discuss credit file impacts.

Ask About My Situation

Can I keep my bank account?

You’ll usually need a basic account (with no overdraft) separate from creditors in your plan. We’ll explain safe banking steps.

How fast can pressure ease?

When protections apply (e.g., Breathing Space), most new enforcement is paused. We notify creditors and agree next steps quickly.
