Help with Moorcroft Debt Recovery

Moorcroft Debt Recovery is one of the UK’s largest and most well-known debt collection agencies. If they are contacting you by phone, letter, or email, it likely means they are chasing a debt on behalf of a lender, or they’ve purchased your debt outright. Either way, you do not need to deal with this alone.

What Is Moorcroft Debt Recovery?

Moorcroft recovers money owed to banks, credit card companies, utility providers, and more. They operate on behalf of big names like BT, O2, Lloyds, and others. They may act as a third-party collector or debt purchaser.

Should You Pay Moorcroft Right Away?

Don't rush into paying. It's important to:

  • Confirm the debt is yours
  • Request a copy of the credit agreement
  • Explore if the debt is legally enforceable
  • Seek free advice from us before agreeing to anything

Can Moorcroft Take You to Court?

Yes, they can initiate County Court proceedings if no action is taken. However, many cases are resolved through affordable repayment plans or formal debt solutions before court becomes necessary.

Solutions We Offer

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement): Combine debts into one payment. Legal protection and interest frozen.
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan): Flexible, informal repayment plans without going to court.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order): Suitable if you meet specific criteria including low income and debt under £30,000.
