IVA vs Other Debt Solutions

IVA vs DMP

An IVA is legally binding and can write off remaining debt on completion; a Debt Management Plan is informal and has no guaranteed write-off.

IVA vs DRO

A Debt Relief Order suits low income and debts under ~¬£30,000. IVAs can suit higher debts with steady income.

IVA vs Bankruptcy

Bankruptcy can be faster but may impact assets more. An IVA can be less disruptive and more flexible, depending on your situation.

IVA Companies ‚Äî Frequently Asked Questions

Do IVA companies charge fees?

Yes, but fees are agreed with creditors and built into your IVA payments ‚Äî no upfront charges.

How long does an IVA last?

Typically 5‚Äì6 years. If you complete the plan, remaining eligible debt is written off.

What changed in 2025 for homeowners?

The revised Protocol (from 1 July 2025) clarifies that where equity release isn‚Äôt suitable, a term extension is used instead of remortgaging, with Protocol IVAs set at 60 months or 72 months if there‚Äôs over ¬£10,000 beneficial interest in a family home.

Will an IVA stop calls and bailiffs?

Once approved, an IVA is legally binding and usually stops enforcement and freezes interest/charges.

Can I check my IVA reference number?

Yes. Your IVA firm will provide your IVA reference ‚Äî keep it for creditor communications.

Which IVA company is best?

The ‚Äúbest‚Äù firm is the one that responds quickly, explains trade-offs clearly, and supports you personally ‚Äî not just the biggest brand.

Are IVA companies regulated?

Insolvency Practitioners (IPs) are authorised by professional bodies (e.g., IPA/ICAEW). Marketing and advice rules also exist ‚Äî see the FCA and ASA resources linked above.

What should I avoid when choosing?

Avoid upfront fees, pressure to sign quickly, and providers who won‚Äôt explain the 2025 homeowner rules in writing.

How fast can an IVA be set up?

It varies. Ask each provider for their typical time to creditor vote and how quickly they reduce creditor contact during the proposal stage.

What if my income drops during an IVA?

Responsible providers have hardship policies (payment holidays, variations, affordability reviews). Ask for these in writing before you choose.

Do the 2025 rules change equity release?

Yes. The 2025 Protocol reduces pressure to remortgage. If you have over ¬£10,000 beneficial interest, a 72-month term may be used instead.

Can I switch IVA company mid-process?

Before approval, switching is simpler. After approval, it may require a formal variation or transfer ‚Äî take advice first to protect your position.

Can I get a free IVA?

Advice can be free, and IVA fees are paid from your plan payments once approved ‚Äî you shouldn‚Äôt be asked to pay fees upfront.
