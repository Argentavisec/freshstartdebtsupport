What Debts Can Be Included in an IVA?

  • Credit cards & store cards
  • Personal loans & overdrafts

IVA vs Other Debt Solutions

IVA vs DMP

An IVA is legally binding and can write off unaffordable debt. A Debt Management Plan is informal, with no guaranteed write-off.

IVA vs DRO

A Debt Relief Order works for debts under ¬£30,000 if you‚Äôre on a low income. An IVA suits higher debts and steady income.

IVA vs Bankruptcy

Bankruptcy can clear debt quicker but impacts assets more heavily. An IVA may be less disruptive and more flexible.
