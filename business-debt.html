Business Debt Help in the UK

Running a business comes with risk, and sometimes cashflow issues, tax arrears, or creditor pressure can become overwhelming. Whether you're self-employed, a sole trader, or running a limited company — support is available.

Common Causes of Business Debt

  • Late customer payments
  • Unexpected bills or emergencies
  • Falling behind on VAT, PAYE or Corporation Tax
  • Loans or overdrafts secured against business assets

What Happens If I Can’t Pay?

You may be contacted by HMRC, commercial lenders, or debt collection firms. Bailiffs and court action are also possible. Early support can prevent this and give you options to take back control.

Business Debt Solutions

Depending on your situation, solutions include:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs)
  • Time To Pay (TTP) arrangements with HMRC
  • Business Debt Management Plans (DMPs)
  • Director advice for limited company liabilities
