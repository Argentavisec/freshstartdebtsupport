Rent Arrears Help in the UK

Are you behind on your rent? You're not alone. Thousands of people in the UK face rent arrears every year — but there are options. With the right support, you may be able to avoid eviction, reduce arrears, and regain control of your finances.

What Are Rent Arrears?

Rent arrears occur when you fall behind on your rent payments. This can lead to pressure from landlords or housing associations, and in some cases, the risk of eviction or legal action.

What Happens If I Ignore Rent Arrears?

  • You may receive a notice seeking possession (eviction notice)
  • Your landlord can take legal action through the courts
  • It can damage your ability to rent in future
  • Your credit rating may be affected

How Can We Help?

Fresh Start Debt Support helps people across the UK tackle rent arrears through practical debt solutions:

  • Negotiate affordable repayment plans
  • Combine arrears with other debts through a DMP or IVA
  • Stop legal action or landlord pressure through formal arrangements

Can Rent Arrears Be Written Off?

In some cases, rent arrears may be included in a formal insolvency arrangement such as an IVA. While not all housing providers accept this, many do — and it can help you clear debts without losing your home.
