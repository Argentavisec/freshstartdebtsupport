Your Options for Barclays Debt: One Affordable Payment

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

An informal plan that pools your non-priority debts (e.g., Barclays card/loan/overdraft plus others) into a single monthly payment you can keep up. Many creditors reduce or freeze interest/charges, though this isn‚Äôt guaranteed. Flexible if income changes.

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

A formal, legally binding agreement based on affordability. If approved and completed successfully, remaining eligible unsecured debt can be written off. Strong protection from most creditor action. Stricter budget rules; variations possible if income changes.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

For low income/low assets. Debts are usually frozen for 12 months and then written off if your situation doesn‚Äôt improve. Strict eligibility applies.
