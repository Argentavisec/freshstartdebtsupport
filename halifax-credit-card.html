Halifax Credit Card Types (At a Glance)

Common categories: 0% balance transfer, 0% purchases, and cards designed to help build credit. The exact APRs, fees and promotional durations change ‚Äî always check the current summary box on the Halifax website.

H3: Balance Transfer Cards

  • Move existing balances from other cards to Halifax
  • Pay a one-off transfer fee; aim to clear before 0% ends

H3: Purchase Cards

  • 0% on new spending for a set period
  • Ideal only if you can clear within the offer window

H3: Credit Builder Cards

  • Designed for limited/poor credit histories
  • Usually higher APRs; keep utilisation low and pay in full if you can

Eligibility & Credit Checks

Halifax will assess your credit history, income and affordability. Multiple hard searches can harm your score. You can review general guidance on improving eligibility via MoneyHelper: how to choose a credit card.

  • Promotional rates end; the card then reverts to the standard purchase/BT APR
