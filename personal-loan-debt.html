Help with Personal Loan Debt

Many people across the UK are struggling to keep up with repayments on personal loans. Whether you borrowed to consolidate previous debt or fund an emergency, interest and charges can quickly get out of hand. We're here to help.

What is Personal Loan Debt?

Personal loan debt is money you owe from borrowing a fixed amount, usually from a bank or lender, which you repay over time with interest. If your financial situation changes, these payments can become unaffordable.

What Happens If I Can’t Pay?

  • Your credit score may be negatively impacted
  • You may receive letters, calls or legal threats from your lender
  • Missed payments could lead to default or County Court Judgments (CCJs)
  • Debt collection agencies may get involved

What Help is Available?

Fresh Start Debt Support helps you explore safe, legal options to manage or even write off unaffordable personal loan debt, including:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – consolidate debt into one monthly payment, freeze interest, write off a portion of debt
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – informal plan to reduce payments and make debt affordable again
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – available for those with low income and little to no assets

Can I Write Off Personal Loan Debt?

Yes. Through an IVA, it may be possible to write off up to 85% of personal loan debt depending on your circumstances. In a DRO, qualifying debts may be cleared after 12 months if your financial situation remains the same.

Why Act Now?

The sooner you get help, the more options you'll have. Waiting can lead to more stress, more fees, and more aggressive creditor contact. Our team will speak to your lenders, negotiate on your behalf, and help you regain control.
