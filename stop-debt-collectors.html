Debt Collectors vs Bailiffs (Enforcement Agents)

Debt collectors are not bailiffs and have no special powers. They cannot enter your home or take goods. Bailiffs (enforcement agents) usually come after a court order (e.g., a CCJ or council tax liability order). For council tax in England & Wales, they cannot force entry on a first visit.

If a bailiff attends, keep doors locked and speak through the door. Offer affordable payments in writing/phone. See Citizens Advice ‚Äî council tax bailiffs.
