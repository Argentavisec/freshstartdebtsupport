Debt Solutions in 2025: Compare DMP, IVA & DRO

There‚Äôs no ‚Äúbest‚Äù solution‚Äîonly the one that fits your income, assets and goals. Start with Compare Solutions then get a personalised view.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

One affordable monthly payment shared across creditors. Many will consider freezing or reducing interest/charges (not guaranteed). Flexible if income varies‚Äîoften a good first stabiliser for 2025 realities.

IVA

Formal, legally binding. You pay what‚Äôs affordable for a set term; remaining eligible unsecured debt may be written off on successful completion. Strong protection from most creditor action once approved.

DRO

For low income/low assets with qualifying debts. Strict criteria apply; debts are usually frozen for 12 months then written off if circumstances don‚Äôt improve.

Debt consolidation vs DMP (UK)

Consolidation is new credit to combine balances; it can be risky if rates rise or affordability is tight. A DMP doesn‚Äôt add new borrowing and can reduce pressure with creditor co-operation. Get independent advice before taking new credit. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
