What Happens If You Don’t Pay NatWest?

Missing payments to NatWest can lead to serious consequences, including:

  • ⚠️ Increased interest, charges, and late fees
  • ⚠️ Default notices on your credit file
  • ⚠️ Debt collection action
  • ⚠️ Court proceedings in severe cases

But the good news is: you have options to stop things before they escalate. We can help you set up a fair, affordable plan that fits your budget — and avoid legal action.

Debt Solutions That Can Help With NatWest

If you're struggling to keep up with your repayments, one of the following solutions might help you:

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – Write off unaffordable debt and protect yourself from legal action
  • Debt Management Plan (DMP) – Freeze interest and reduce your monthly payments
  • Debt Relief Order (DRO) – Clear debt in 12 months if you meet certain criteria
  • Compare all debt solutions to find the best fit

Our small UK-based team can help you understand which route works for your personal situation.

Is Your NatWest Debt With a Collection Agency?

If NatWest has passed your account to a third-party collector, such as Cabot Financial, PRA Group, or Resolvecall, don't panic. These agencies still have to follow the rules — and many debts can be paused, reduced, or included in an arrangement.

We can contact them on your behalf and help stop stressful calls, letters, or threats of legal action.
