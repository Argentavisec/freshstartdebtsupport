Payday Loan Debt Help

Struggling with payday loans? You're not alone. Many people in the UK turn to payday loans for short-term relief, only to be hit with extremely high interest rates and aggressive collection tactics.

Why Are Payday Loans So Dangerous?

  • APR can exceed 1,000%, making balances skyrocket fast
  • Most lenders demand full repayment by your next payday
  • Late payments lead to fees, charges, and collections

Can Payday Loans Be Included in Debt Help?

Yes! Payday loans can be included in a formal solution like an IVA or DMP. These plans:

  • Stop interest and charges
  • Freeze creditor contact
  • Allow one affordable monthly payment

How Can Fresh Start Help?

We provide expert advice and help you understand which plan is right for you — including options to reduce or write off debt based on your situation.
