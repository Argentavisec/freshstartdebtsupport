Payday Loan Debt Help in the UK

Payday loans may seem like a quick fix, but they often come with sky-high interest rates and tight repayment deadlines. Many UK borrowers quickly find themselves caught in a debt spiral.

Why Are Payday Loans Dangerous?

  • Annual interest rates often exceed 1,000%
  • Fees for missed payments are common
  • Repayments are taken directly from your bank account

Can I Write Off Payday Loan Debt?

If your financial situation qualifies, an IVA or DRO may help write off a portion of your payday loan debt. Even with a DMP, interest and fees can be frozen.

Will Payday Lenders Stop Contacting Me?

Once enrolled in a formal plan, your creditors—including payday lenders—must communicate through your debt advisor, not directly with you.
