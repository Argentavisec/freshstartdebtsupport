Help with Fredrickson International

Fredrickson International is a debt collection agency that may be contacting you by letter, phone, or email regarding unpaid debts. If you're feeling overwhelmed or unsure how to respond — we can help you take control.

Who Are Fredrickson International?

Fredrickson is part of the Lowell Group and acts as a debt collection agency on behalf of a wide range of companies, including banks, telecom firms, credit card providers, and utility suppliers. They may be chasing debt they’ve bought, or one they’ve been asked to recover.

Should I Pay Fredrickson Immediately?

Before you agree to pay, it’s essential to:

  • Confirm the debt is valid and yours
  • Ask for a copy of the original credit agreement
  • Check if the debt is statute-barred (too old to be enforced)
  • Get free, confidential advice from our team before making any decisions

Can Fredrickson Take Me to Court?

Yes, they can escalate a case to court, which might result in a County Court Judgment (CCJ). However, court is often avoidable with the right advice and a suitable debt solution in place.

What Can I Do About Fredrickson?

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement): An agreement that combines your debts into one affordable monthly payment and stops legal action.
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan): Reduce monthly payments with no court involvement.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order): For people on low income with debts under £30,000 and limited assets.
