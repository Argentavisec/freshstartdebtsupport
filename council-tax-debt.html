Why Council Tax Arrears Are a Priority (and What That Means)

Council tax debt is a priority debt. Councils can obtain a Liability Order relatively quickly, add fees, and instruct enforcement agents. They can also arrange deductions from wages or benefits. That’s why acting early is essential.

  • ⚖️ Liability Order may be obtained through the magistrates’ court
  • 🚪 Enforcement agents (bailiffs) can be instructed if arrears persist
  • 💷 Deductions from wages/benefits can reduce your income unexpectedly
  • 🚨 Committal proceedings are rare but possible for wilful refusal

Early action doesn’t have to be complicated. Create breathing room, then build a plan that fits your budget and life.

Debt Solutions That Can Include Council Tax Arrears

Many people with council tax debt also have credit cards, loans or overdrafts. Combining everything into one plan can simplify life and reduce stress.

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – a single affordable monthly payment with legal protection. Any remaining eligible unsecured debt may be written off at the end.
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – an informal plan to lower payments; many creditors may reduce or freeze interest and charges.
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – for people on low income with low assets; debts are frozen for 12 months then written off if circumstances don’t improve.

Bailiffs & Council Tax Debt: Your Rights and Quick Wins

With a Liability Order, councils can pass accounts to enforcement agents. You still have rights:

  • They cannot force entry on a first visit for council tax—do not let them in.
  • You can keep communication in writing and propose affordable payments.
  • Stopping visits quickly reduces added enforcement fees.

Council Tax Debt FAQs

Can council tax debt be written off?

Yes—typically through a formal route such as an IVA or a DRO, depending on eligibility and your wider circumstances.

Can bailiffs force entry for council tax?

Not on a first visit for council tax. Peaceful entry is required. Keep doors closed, communicate in writing, and get support quickly. See our overview of Jacobs Enforcement, Bristow & Sutor and others listed above.

Is Breathing Space right for council tax arrears?

Often, yes. It pauses most enforcement while you work with us on a sustainable plan. Read more: Breathing Space.

Should I prioritise council tax over credit cards?

Yes. Council tax debt is priority. Then reduce other payments through an IVA, DMP or other affordable plan.

Does a council tax payment plan affect my credit file?

Council tax is not usually reported to credit agencies. However, entering a formal solution (IVA/DRO) will affect credit for a period. The trade-off is stability and protection from enforcement.

Can this include other debts like utilities or rent?

Yes, we commonly include Utility Bills, Rent Arrears, Credit Card Debt and more into one affordable structure.
