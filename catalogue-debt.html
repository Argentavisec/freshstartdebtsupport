Missed payments and defaults can impact your credit file. Formal solutions (IVA/DRO) also affect credit for a periodâ€”but they can provide stability, stop pressure and create a clear route to recovery.

Many creditors will freeze or reduce interest in a DMP. In an IVA, interest is typically frozen and you pay an agreed affordable amount each month.
