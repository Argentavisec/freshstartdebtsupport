Help with Catalogue Debt in the UK

Falling behind on catalogue payments? You’re not alone. Catalogue debt often starts small but can spiral out of control due to high interest, late fees, and constant creditor contact. If you're struggling, Fresh Start Debt Support can help you reduce payments, freeze interest, and regain control.

Check If I Qualify

What Happens If You Miss Catalogue Payments?

  • Late fees and increased interest charges
  • Account referred to debt collectors like Lowell
  • Default notices and damage to your credit score
  • Legal action if the debt remains unpaid

Common catalogue providers include Very, Littlewoods, and JD Williams — all of which can pass your account to external recovery agencies quickly.

Can Catalogue Debt Be Written Off?

Yes. If you qualify for an IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement), a significant portion of your catalogue debt can be legally written off. You'll make one affordable payment each month for a fixed term, usually 5 years — and the rest may be cleared after that.

If an IVA isn’t suitable, a Debt Management Plan (DMP) or Debt Relief Order (DRO) might still allow you to lower payments and freeze interest.

What Support Will I Get?

  • We deal with creditors and collectors on your behalf
  • Interest and charges can be frozen in most cases
  • Legal contact and harassment from collectors will stop
  • We’ll find a plan that works for your unique situation

Even if you have multiple debts — such as credit cards, payday loans, or overdrafts — they can be combined into one lower monthly payment.

Will My Catalogue Company Stop Contacting Me?

Yes. Once a formal plan is in place, like an IVA or DMP, all contact from lenders and collectors should stop. You are legally protected from further harassment, and we handle the communication for you.
