Catalogue Debt Help in the UK

Catalogue debt can build up fast, especially when missed payments trigger late fees and interest. Many UK households rely on catalogues for essential purchases — but what happens when repayments become unmanageable?

What Happens If You Fall Behind?

  • Penalty charges and added fees
  • Interest rates increasing without notice
  • Referral to debt collection agencies
  • Negative entries on your credit file

How Fresh Start Debt Support Can Help

We help individuals across the UK deal with catalogue debt through tailored solutions like IVAs and DMPs:

  • Combine multiple catalogue debts into one manageable monthly payment
  • Freeze interest and stop creditor contact
  • Possibly write off unaffordable debt
See If You Qualify

Can I Write Off Catalogue Debt?

In many cases, yes — especially through an IVA. If you owe over £6,000 in total unsecured debt and can't afford repayments, we may help you legally write off some of the amount owed.

Will I Still Receive Letters and Calls?

No. Once you enter a supported plan, your creditors are legally required to stop contacting you directly. We handle communications on your behalf.
