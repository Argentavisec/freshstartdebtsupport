Who do Klarna use for debt collection?

Missed payments can be passed to agencies such as Debt Recovery Plus (DRP), Intrum UK, Pastdue Credit Solutions, Controlaccount or Arvato Financial Solutions. Expect letters, emails and calls. If ignored, they may recommend legal action—but you still have strong options.

What to do when a collector writes to you

Keep doors closed to bailiffs (only after court). Reply in writing, not on-the-spot calls. Propose what you can really afford and ask about Breathing Space to pause interest/most enforcement for 60 days while we set things up.

Fast de-escalation steps

  1. List income/essential bills to set a safe payment.
  2. Enter Breathing Space (if eligible).
  3. Choose DMP/IVA/DRO for longer-term protection.

Can Klarna take you to court?

Yes—if arrears persist, a claim can be issued for a County Court Judgment (CCJ). A CCJ can hurt your credit for six years and lead to enforcement (bailiffs, attachments). Acting early matters: formal solutions can halt claims and freeze interest.

If you’ve had a “Letter of Claim”

Respond within the deadline and talk to us the same day. We’ll help you evidence an affordable budget and move into a route that protects you.

What if I already have a CCJ?

You can still propose affordable payments (and sometimes vary them). But it’s easier to prevent a CCJ by acting sooner.

Avoid a Klarna CCJ with an IVA

Breathing Space: pause interest, charges & most enforcement

The Debt Respite Scheme gives you up to 60 days where most interest/fees and enforcement are paused. That space lets us verify your budget (via statements) and set up a DMP/IVA/DRO without constant pressure.

Who qualifies?

People in problem debt in England & Wales, via an approved adviser. There’s also a Mental Health Crisis route with longer protection.

What Breathing Space does not do

It doesn’t erase debt by itself; it buys time to put the right structure in place.

Include Klarna in an IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

An IVA is a legally binding plan (usually 5–6 years) with one affordable monthly payment. Interest/charges are frozen and, at completion, remaining eligible unsecured debt can be written off.

Good signs for IVA suitability

  • Total unsecured debts typically £6,000+
  • Regular income to support a monthly contribution
  • Multiple creditors (e.g., Klarna + cards/catalogues)

Can I use Klarna during an IVA?

Generally no new credit over £500 without your insolvency practitioner’s permission.

Check IVA Eligibility

Include Klarna in a DMP (Debt Management Plan)

A DMP is an informal plan with one affordable payment shared across creditors. Many lenders freeze or reduce interest/charges when shown a fair, evidence-based budget (not guaranteed).

Direct Klarna plan vs DMP

Direct plan: only Klarna, quick to set up. DMP: covers Klarna and other debts in one payment, reducing admin and the risk of new arrears elsewhere.

Set Up a Klarna DMP

Low income? Klarna may be covered by a DRO

A Debt Relief Order (strict eligibility) freezes debts for 12 months and then writes them off if your situation hasn’t improved. Suits low income/low assets up to the current DRO limits.

Why people choose DRO

  • No ongoing repayments
  • All eligible debts covered together (incl. Klarna)
  • Clean finish if circumstances remain the same
See If I Qualify for a DRO

Bankruptcy and Klarna

As unsecured credit, Klarna is included in bankruptcy. It’s a serious step with restrictions but can be the cleanest reset when debts are unmanageable and income is limited.

Key points

  • Debts usually discharged after 12 months
  • Possible income payment agreements if surplus exists
  • Credit and practical restrictions during the period

I’ve Been Passed to Collectors — Help

Bailiffs for Klarna — can they come to my home?

Not without a court order. Bailiffs only attend after a CCJ and enforcement. Early action (Breathing Space or a suitable plan) usually prevents this path entirely. Keep doors closed, communicate in writing, and get advice before you respond to any threats.

Protect yourself

Don’t panic, don’t let strangers in, and don’t pay on the doorstep. Ask us to speak to creditors for you and stabilise the situation first.

Does Klarna show on your credit file — and for how long?

It depends on the product and reporting. Missed payments and CCJs are the most damaging. A CCJ typically remains on your file for six years. If you’re already behind, moving into a credible plan can prevent escalation and show you’re addressing the debt.

How to limit damage

  • Prioritise essentials, then set one affordable payment across all debts
  • Use Breathing Space to pause pressure while you organise
  • Choose a sustainable route (DMP/IVA/DRO)
