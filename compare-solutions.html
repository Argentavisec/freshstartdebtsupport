What Types of Debt Can Be Included?

Debt solutions like IVAs, DMPs, and DROs can help you reduce and manage a wide range of unsecured debts. These may include:

  • Credit card debt
  • Personal loans
  • Catalogue debts
  • Council tax arrears
  • Utility bill debt (gas, electric, water)
  • Payday loans
  • Bank overdrafts
  • Store cards
  • HMRC debts
  • Rent arrears
  • Gambling debts

These plans typically cannot include secured debts like mortgages or car finance agreements. However, our team can advise on how these may be managed alongside your plan.

What Happens After You Start a Debt Plan?

Once your debt solution begins — whether it's an IVA, Debt Management Plan, or Debt Relief Order — you’ll experience immediate relief from debt pressures:

  • ✅ Creditor contact usually stops — no more constant letters or calls
  • ✅ Interest and charges may be frozen on eligible debts
  • ✅ Legal action is paused for IVA and DRO applicants
  • ✅ One affordable monthly payment instead of multiple demands

You'll regain control of your finances, know exactly what’s being paid, and have peace of mind with a clear path forward. Over time, you may also improve your budgeting and reduce money stress — creating healthier financial habits for the future.
