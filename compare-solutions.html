Quick Overview of Each Debt Option

  • IVA – Legally write off unaffordable debt and pay what you can afford over 5–6 years.
  • DMP – Informal payment plan to repay debts over time, usually with frozen interest.
  • DRO – Debt relief for those on low income with no assets; debts are cleared after 12 months.
  • Bankruptcy – Clears debts quickly, but can impact your job and home depending on your situation.

Which Debt Solution Is Right for Me?

The right debt solution depends on your individual situation. If you:

  • Have over £6,000 of unsecured debt and a steady income, an IVA may be ideal.
  • Can afford to repay debts in full but need lower monthly payments, a DMP might work best.
  • Are on a low income with little to no assets, consider a DRO.
  • Have no other way out and need a clean slate, then Bankruptcy could be the last resort.
