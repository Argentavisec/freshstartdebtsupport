Compare IVA vs DMP vs Bankruptcy

If you're struggling with debt, it's crucial to understand the options available. Below is a comparison of the three most common solutions: IVA, DMP, and Bankruptcy.

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

  • Legal agreement to pay part of your debt over 5–6 years
  • Remaining debt is written off after term ends
  • Stops interest and legal action
  • Impacts credit rating for 6 years

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

  • Informal agreement with creditors to pay off debt monthly
  • No legal protection from creditors
  • Flexible and easier to change
  • Interest may still accrue

Bankruptcy

  • Legal process to write off unmanageable debt
  • Assets may be sold to repay creditors
  • Lasts about 12 months
  • Strong negative impact on credit
