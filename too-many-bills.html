Common Debt Solutions for People with Too Many Bills

If you have too many bills, several UK debt solutions might help reduce your monthly payments and write off some debts. Here are the most common options:

Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

An IVA is a formal, legally binding agreement where you make one affordable payment monthly based on your budget. After 5-6 years, any remaining qualifying debts are usually written off. IVAs protect you from bailiffs and court actions.

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

A DMP is an informal arrangement with creditors to pay what you can afford monthly. Interest and fees may be frozen but debts are not written off. Itâ€™s flexible and ideal for those with irregular income.

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

If you have a low income and limited assets, a DRO can freeze your debts for 12 months. If your financial situation doesnâ€™t improve, debts can be written off completely. DROs are quick, affordable, and often suitable for those overwhelmed by bills.
