Fresh Start IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

A Fresh Start IVA allows you to consolidate your unsecured debts into one affordable monthly payment. It stops legal action, freezes interest, and writes off a significant portion of what you owe — usually after five or six years.

If you're eligible, this legally binding solution protects you from bailiffs and court orders. At Fresh Start Debt Support, we help you determine if an IVA is suitable and connect you with a licensed Insolvency Practitioner.

Find Out If I Qualify for a Fresh Start IVA

Fresh Start DMP (Debt Management Plan)

A Fresh Start DMP is an informal agreement to repay your debts at a pace you can afford. We contact your creditors and negotiate lower payments — often with interest frozen — without the need for court involvement.

It’s ideal if your situation is temporary or improving. Our team can set up a tailored DMP to ease pressure, reduce monthly bills, and keep you on track financially without added stress.

Set Up a Fresh Start DMP

Fresh Start Debt Relief Order (DRO)

A Fresh Start DRO is designed for people with low income and minimal assets. It freezes your debt for 12 months — and if your situation doesn’t improve, the debts are written off completely at the end of that period.

We’ll check your eligibility, prepare your application, and submit everything through an authorised intermediary. If you qualify, a DRO can be a powerful way to close the chapter on debt quickly and safely.

Apply for a Fresh Start DRO

Fresh Start Bankruptcy Support

Bankruptcy is often seen as a last resort — but for some people, it’s the cleanest way to wipe out unmanageable debt. Fresh Start Bankruptcy Support ensures you understand the process, risks, and long-term implications before making a decision.

We help you apply, guide you through each step, and explore whether a less severe alternative like a DRO or IVA might be more suitable.

Get Bankruptcy Help from Fresh Start

Fresh Start Trust Deed (Scotland Only)

Live in Scotland? A Fresh Start Trust Deed is the Scottish version of an IVA. It lets you consolidate debt, protect assets, and potentially write off unaffordable balances after 4 years.

Our Scottish team will assess your situation and introduce you to a qualified Insolvency Practitioner who specialises in Trust Deeds and Sequestration (Scottish bankruptcy).
