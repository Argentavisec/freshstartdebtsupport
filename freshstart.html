What Does “Fresh Start” Mean in Debt Help?

A plain-English definition

Fresh Start is not a single product. It’s the outcome: a realistic plan that stops the spiral (interest/charges or most enforcement where applicable), replaces juggling bills with one affordable payment, and gives you a clear finish line. We deliver this using the solution that best fits your life — not the one that pays the most commission.

Fresh Start outcomes we aim for

  • 🛑 Reduced collections pressure and enforcement (where a formal solution applies)
  • 💷 One sustainable monthly amount based on a fair household budget
  • ⏳ A set timeframe to become debt-free
  • 🔐 Named support and variations if your situation changes

Solutions We Use to Deliver Your Fresh Start

Fresh Start IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

Consolidate unsecured debts into one affordable monthly payment. Stops most enforcement once approved, freezes interest, and can write off unaffordable balances at completion.

Learn more: IVA guide • Compare with DMP/DRO

See If I Qualify for an IVA

Fresh Start DMP (Debt Management Plan)

Informal plan with reduced payments. Creditors often freeze/reduce interest voluntarily. Good when your income may improve.

Read: DMP explained

Set Up a DMP

Fresh Start DRO (Debt Relief Order)

For low income/low assets within set limits. Debts are frozen for 12 months and then written off if circumstances don’t improve.

Check: DRO eligibility & rules

Check DRO Eligibility

Fresh Start Bankruptcy Support

Sometimes the cleanest reset. We explain implications, help you apply, and compare with less severe options if suitable.

Detail: Bankruptcy guide

Get Bankruptcy Help

Fresh Start Trust Deed (Scotland)

Scottish alternative to an IVA (usually 4 years). We connect you with a Scottish IP for Trust Deeds or Sequestration.
