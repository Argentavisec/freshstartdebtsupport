Help with Moriarty Law Debt

Receiving a letter from Moriarty Law can feel intimidating — especially if it includes a threat of legal action. Moriarty Law is a UK firm that often works on behalf of debt collectors such as Lowell, and they are known for issuing County Court Claim forms.

Who Are Moriarty Law?

Moriarty Law LLP are solicitors who act on behalf of creditors and debt purchasers. If they are contacting you, it means they may be looking to take legal steps to recover an unpaid debt.

What Can They Do?

  • Send a Letter Before Action (LBA)
  • Issue a County Court Claim (CCJ)
  • Apply for enforcement such as bailiffs or an Attachment of Earnings Order

Should I Ignore Them?

No. Ignoring Moriarty Law could result in a default CCJ being issued. It's important to understand your options and take action early. We can help you resolve the debt and avoid court proceedings if possible.

Solutions That Can Help

  • IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement) – Stop legal action and repay debt through one affordable monthly payment
  • DMP (Debt Management Plan) – Lower your payments and freeze charges without going to court
  • DRO (Debt Relief Order) – Suitable if you owe under £30,000 and meet certain criteria
